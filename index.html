About Warsjawa

Conference for developers, by developers. Unique 100% workshop formula. “Learn by doing” approach. Proudly host workshops related to all aspects of software development: designing, developing, testing, maintaining etc. Initially oriented around Java and JVM programming languages. Now open to other programming languages like Scala, Groovy, Python, mobile development for Android, iOS and others.

Being open, resulted with many fascinating tracks related to databases, big data frameworks, agile and product management methodologies and system administration. Thus that, peoples from product owners by developers to devops, can find anything for themselves.

It is free of charge for all participants. Last year it brought more than 550 registered attendees from all over Poland, who were practicing their skills within 23 parallel tracks! This year, we are aiming even higher. Moving warsjawa to completly new level.
